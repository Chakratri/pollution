Pollution Control

Type of pollution How to control
Air pollution Use craft supplies in well-ventilated areas.

Make sure your gas stove is well-ventilated.

Noise pollution Shut the Door when using noisy Machines.

Go Green by planning trees.

Water pollution Avoid using a garbage disposal.

Keep solid wastes solid. Make a compost pile from vegetable scraps.

Soil pollution Buy biodegradable products.

Store all liquid chemicals and waste in spill-proof containers.
